About

Weekly Bard is tool for any musician to practice their craft through weekly competitions. Additionaly, Weekly Bard is a place to encourage and learn from fellow musicians. Whether you're new or old to songwriting, you can always learn and lend a hand.

How it Works

Every Monday, a new theme is selected, and members have until the following Monday to write and submit their piece. Additonaly, members of the community can vote on submissions from the previous week and future themes! While there's no prize for winning, winners may submit new theme ideas and their submissions will be featured in a community post.

Rules

There's not many hard rules, but in order to maintain a welcoming atmosphere, we ask that members keep discourse friendly, accepting, and family-friendly

Additionaly, please refrain from:

  • Using copyrighted works which you do not have rights for.
  • Submitting NSFW thumbnails, titles, descriptions, etc.
  • Third rule because three looks better
